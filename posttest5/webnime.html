Popular Character Anime

Eren Yeager

Eren Yeager is the main protagonist of the Attack on Titan series. He is a former member of the Survey Corps, the leader of the Yeagerists

Tanjiro Kamado

Tanjiro Kamado is the main protagonist of Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba. He is a Demon Slayer in the Demon Slayer Corps

Gojo Satoru

Gojo Satoru is one of the main protagonists of the Jujutsu Kaisen series. He is a special grade jujutsu sorcerer and widely recognized as the strongest in the world.

Chitoge Kirisaki

Chitoge Kirisaki is the main female deuteragonist of the Nisekoi series. She is who served as the fake girlfriend of Raku Ichijō for the next three years of her school life to prevent a war from starting between their families.

Anos Voldigoad

Anos Voldigoad is a powerful demon who was known as the Demon King of Tyranny (暴虐の魔王 Bōgyaku no Maō?) during the Mythical Age, infamous for his absolute power and extreme brutality towards his enemies.

Uchiha Sasuke

Uchiha Sasuke is one of the last surviving members of Konohagakure's Uchiha clan. After his older brother, Itachi, slaughtered their clan

Katsuki Bakugo

Roronoa Zoro

Roronoa Zoro also known as "Pirate Hunter" Zoro,[9] is a main combatant of the Straw Hat Pirates, one of their two swordsmen, one of the Senior Officers of the Straw Hat Grand Fleet

Monkey D.Luffy

Monkey D.Luffy also known as "Straw Hat Luffy" and commonly as "Straw Hat",[10] is the founder and captain of the increasingly infamous and powerful Straw Hat Pirates, as well as the most powerful of its top fighters

Uzumaki Naruto

Naruto Uzumaki is a shinobi of Konohagakure's Uzumaki clan. He became the jinchūriki of the Nine-Tails on the day of his birth — a fate that caused him to be shunned by most of Konoha throughout his childhood

Raku Ichijou

Raku Ichijou is the male protagonist of the Nisekoi series that is written and illustrated by Naoshi Komi. He serves as the fake boyfriend of Chitoge Kirisaki for the next three years of his life to prevent a war between the Bee Hive Gang and the Shūei Clan before they eventually get married.

Chika Fujiawara

chika Fujiwara is one of the protagonists in the Kaguya-sama wa Kokurasetai series. She was a third-year high school student at Shuchi'in Academy[2] , a member of the Tabletop Gaming Club and the secretary of the 67th and 68th Student Council.
